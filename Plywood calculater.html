How It Works

This calculator determines how many standard plywood sheets (122cm × 244cm) you need to cover a given area. The calculation accounts for the fact that plywood sheets are typically cut slightly smaller than their nominal size to account for wastage and gaps.

Calculation Formula:

Plywood sheets along length = (Length / 14.75) - 1

Plywood sheets along width = (Width / 15.70) - 1

Total plywood sheets = (Plywood along length) × (Plywood along width)

The formulas account for standard plywood sheet dimensions and typical construction practices where sheets are cut slightly smaller than the nominal size.
